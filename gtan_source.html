Pre-seed to Series A Canadian tech investing since 2008.

GTAN is a community of angels, super angels, family offices, and venture fundspowering Waterloo Regions innovation economy.

We are highly collaborative making us Canadas most active early-stage investors.

Collectively, weve invested in 250+ startups spanning many industries with software, hardware, deep tech, medtech, quantum, and more.

Fundraising

We have a couple paths for founders looking for venture grade investment.

Pitch Path

For fast moving founders with novel IP and a large vision.

Angel, pre-seed & seed stage rounds of $750k-$5MM

Minimum of $300k left on the round

B2B is preferred

Canadian tech companies

Ready for due diligence

Apply for Funding

We currently have a 66% success rate for companies that pitch at our main events.

While each of our investors writes their own cheques, collectively we are averaging $350k per investment.

Accelerated Path

For fast moving rounds with a strong & credible lead investor secured.

We are Canadas fastest top-up and layer-on capital program.

Averaging $500k (up to $1.5M) within 3 weeks from pitching

Typically seed to A rounds no larger than $15M and sub $50MM valuation

Ask your Canadian investor to refer

This is a highly selective path with a nearly 100% success rate.

Startup Visa Path

For non-Canadian ventures looking for a Startup Visa.

GTAN is an accredited angel group for SUV investments. We work with partners like Roseview Global Accelerator to help with the process.

A history of impact

Waterloo Region is Canadas technology hub; a community of builders and innovators, tech visionaries and investors, and our home.

$150M of local angel capital has been invested into over 250 companies by GTANs members.

These companies have raised >$1B in follow-on capital, leading to asymmetrical economic and financial impact.
