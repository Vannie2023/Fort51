Platform

  • Enterprise Risk Heat Map & Residual Risk View

    Visualizes inherent and residual risks across the organization.

  • Centralized Risk Register with AI Intelligent Categorization

    Maintains a single source of truth for all risks, categorized by risk type, business line, geography, customer segment, and delivery channel.

  • Control Effectiveness & Gap Assessment

    Evaluates preventive and detective controls against identified risks, highlights control weaknesses, and flags gaps requiring remediation.

  • Dynamic Risk Scoring & Trend Analytics

    Continuously recalculates risk scores and tracks trends over time to demonstrate how risks evolve and how mitigation efforts reduce exposure.

  • Action Plans, Ownership & Audit-Ready Evidence

    Assigns remediation actions to owners, tracks progress, and maintains a complete audit trail for regulatory examinations and internal audits.

AI Admin Panel Features

  • Risk Recommendations & Explanations

    It doesn’t just score risk — it explains why. The AI surfaces recommended risk ratings, control gaps, and mitigation actions, with plain-language explanations regulators can understand.

  • Intelligent User & Role Management

    No more manual permission headaches. Admins can assign roles (Risk Owner, Compliance Officer, Reviewer, Auditor) with AI-suggested access based on job function and regulatory best practices.

  • AI-Generated Executive & Regulator Reports

    Reporting goes from days to minutes. One-click generation of board-ready and regulator-ready reports, with AI-written summaries explaining risk posture, trends, and remediation status.

  • Smart Alerts & Risk Change Detection

    It catches issues before examiners do. AI continuously monitors risk data, controls, and inputs and flags material changes—new products, geographies, control failures, or regulatory shifts.

User Roles, Permissions & Access — Made Effortless

  • Pre-Built Regulatory Role Templates

    Instantly assign roles like Admin, Risk Owner, Compliance Officer, Reviewer, and Auditor—pre-mapped to best-practice permissions.

  • AI-Recommended Permissions

    AI suggests least-privilege access based on role, behavior, and regulatory norms—reducing access risk and setup time.

  • Granular, Risk-Based Access Controls

    Control access by module, risk category, geography, business line, or data sensitivity—without complex configuration.

  • Automated Access Reviews & Audit Trail

  • Centralized User Management Dashboard

About Fort51

Our team brings more than 20 years of hands-on experience in AML/ATF risk management, gained through direct work with regulators and leading financial institutions. We understand how AML risk assessments are examined, challenged, and relied upon by supervisory authorities—and why manual, spreadsheet-driven processes are increasingly slow, frustrating, error-filled and difficult to sustain.

Through years of delivering AML risk assessments using proven methodologies and structured risk-scoring models, we’ve developed deep insight into what compliance teams truly need: a practical, intuitive, do-it-yourself platform that reduces effort and time while maintaining regulatory rigor. This led to the development of the Fort51 AI AML Risk Management platform, designed to automate key assessment workflows and significantly accelerate delivery timelines.

We’ve partnered closely with clients through in-depth interviews and step-by-step walkthroughs to design workflows that reflect real-world compliance operations. Combined with our deep understanding of global AML regulations, industry best practices, and nuanced risk-scoring requirements, Fort51 enables faster, more cost-effective AML risk assessments with clear, consistent, and regulator-ready outcomes.
